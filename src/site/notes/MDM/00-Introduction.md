# Might, Daring & Magic
## Introduction

---

### What Is This Game?

Might, Daring & Magic (MDM) is a tabletop roleplaying game built for sword & sorcery adventure. It is fast, dangerous, and fun. Characters are capable but mortal. Magic is rare and feared. Combat is quick and brutal. The world rewards boldness and punishes complacency.

MDM uses a 3d6 roll-under system. You roll three six-sided dice and try to get equal to or under a target stat. Lower rolls are better. High stats keep you alive.

---

### Design Philosophy

- **Fast over fiddly.** Rules exist to create tension and drama, not to simulate reality.
- **Dangerous by default.** Characters can die. That's what makes survival meaningful.
- **Magic is rare.** One mage in a hundred people. It should feel like that at the table.
- **The fiction first.** Roll dice when the outcome is uncertain and the stakes are real. Don't roll for things that don't matter.
- **GMs set the tone.** The rules give you a framework. The table gives it life.

---

### What You Need

- 3-6 players (1 GM, 2-5 players)
- Multiple d6s (primary dice), plus d4, d8, d10 for some weapon/spell results
- Character sheets
- Something to write with
