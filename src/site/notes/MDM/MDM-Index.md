## Quick Reference

### Core Mechanic
Roll **3d6 under your stat**. Lower is better.

### Stats
| Stat | Name |
|------|------|
| ST | Strength |
| DX | Dexterity |
| IQ | Intelligence |
| WL | Willpower |
| LK | Luck |

### Character Creation Summary
1. Choose race → [[MDM/06-Races\|06-Races]]
2. Distribute +3 stat points (min 6, max 14)
3. HP = ST | SP = IQ (mages only)
4. Spend 5 skill points → [[MDM/03-Skills\|03-Skills]]
5. Choose spells if mage → [[MDM/05-Magic\|05-Magic]]
6. Buy equipment → [[MDM/07-Equipment\|07-Equipment]]
7. Note 2 Fate Points
8. Optional: take advantages/disadvantages/quirks → [[MDM/15-Advantages-Disadvantages\|15-Advantages-Disadvantages]]

### LK Threshold
| LK | Effect |
|----|--------|
| 10+ | Favorable |
| 9 or below | Unfavorable |

### Combat Summary
- **Hit:** Roll 3d6 under DX
- **Damage:** Flat weapon dice
- **Armor:** Flat DR reduction
- **0 HP:** Unconscious, bleed 1/turn
- **Negative ST:** Dead

### Races at a Glance
| Race | Mods | Ability |
|------|------|---------|
| Human | — | +1 skill point |
| Ashborn | ST+1, LK-1 | Ember Blood |
| Tidemarked | ST-1, IQ+1, WL+1 | Still Water |
| Greyborn | WL+1, LK-1 | Threshold |
