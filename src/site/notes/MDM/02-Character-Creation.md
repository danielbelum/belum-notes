# Character Creation

---

## Step by Step

### 1. Choose Your Race
Select one of the four available lineages. Each provides stat modifiers and one racial ability. See [[MDM/06-Races\|06-Races]].

### 2. Set Your Stats
All stats begin at **10**. You have **+3 bonus points** to distribute as you choose.

**Limits at creation:**
- Minimum: 6
- Maximum: 14 (before racial modifiers)

Apply racial stat modifiers after distributing your bonus points.

### 3. Calculate HP
**HP = your final ST score.**

### 4. Calculate Spell Points (Mages Only)
**SP = your final IQ score.**

Non-mages have no SP.

### 5. Assign Skills
You start with **5 skill points**. Each skill costs 1 point.

- You may take any skill once at creation
- Untrained skills can still be attempted at **-2 to the linked stat**
- See [[MDM/03-Skills\|03-Skills]] for the full list

### 6. Choose Spells (Mages Only)
If your character is a mage, select spells up to your IQ minimum. You must meet the IQ requirement for each spell. See [[MDM/05-Magic\|05-Magic]].

### 7. Choose Equipment
Select starting gear from the equipment list. The GM may provide a starting package or a budget.

**Default starting budget:** 10 gold pieces.

### 8. Note Fate Points
All characters start with **2 Fate Points**.

### 9. Record Derived Values

| Value | Calculation |
|-------|-------------|
| HP | = ST |
| SP | = IQ (mages only) |
| Fate Points | 2 (all characters) |
| Initiative modifier | LK 13-14: +1 / LK 6-9: -1 / else: 0 |
