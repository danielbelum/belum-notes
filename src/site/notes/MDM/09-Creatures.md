# Monsters

---

## How to Use This Section

**Mooks** have ST, DX, and IQ only. They act as groups. Roll once for the group per round.

**Bosses** have full stats (ST, DX, IQ, WL, LK) and unique abilities. They act individually.

**Spellcasting monsters** have SP and full spell descriptions inline.

---

## Humanoids — Low Threat

> **Goblin** | Mook | Threat: Low
> ST 7 | DX 10 | IQ 8 | HP 7
> Armor: Leather (DR 1) | Weapon: Dagger (1d6) or Short Sword (1d6+1)
> *Cowardly — flees if half their group is down*

> **Bandit** | Mook | Threat: Low
> ST 9 | DX 10 | IQ 9 | HP 9
> Armor: Leather (DR 1) | Weapon: Broadsword (2d6)
> *Surrenders if clearly outmatched*

> **Kobold** | Mook | Threat: Low
> ST 6 | DX 12 | IQ 7 | HP 6
> Armor: None | Weapon: Spear (2d6)
> *Never fights alone — always in groups of 4+*
