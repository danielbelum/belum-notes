## Starting Equipment Packages

Players may choose a starting package instead of purchasing individually. Starting budget is otherwise **10gp**.

### Warrior Package
- Broadsword (1gp), Chain armor (8gp), Round shield (3sp), Dagger (2sp), Backpack, Bedroll, Waterskin, 5 days rations, Flint & Steel — ~3sp remaining

### Scout Package
- Short bow (5sp) + 24 arrows (24cp), Hand axe (4sp), Leather armor (2gp), Rope (1sp), Grappling hook (5sp), Backpack, Bedroll, Waterskin, 4 days rations — ~2sp remaining

### Mage Package
- Dagger (2sp), Heavy cloak, Spell journal, Ink & quill (3sp), Parchment x6 (3cp), Backpack, Bedroll, Waterskin, 3 days rations — ~4sp remaining

### Rogue Package
- Short sword (6sp), Dagger (2sp), Lockpicks (5sp), Leather armor (2gp), Dark clothing (1sp), Backpack, Bedroll, Waterskin, 3 days rations — ~3sp remaining
